9. (Worksheet on Canvas) Estimation. No computers/looking up allowed bc the goal is to practice a skill that is useful when we don’t have real-world data! Divide the students into teams of 3-4. Each team starts on one of the first seven estimates with instructions to move on to the next one when done, circling back to Google search if they reach the end of the list. The teams start in different places, so after about half an hour the class has found two or three estimates for each of the seven tasks. The different answers for each task should have the same order of magnitude. If they don’t, we try to figure out why not. (Good to have poster paper or whiteboards for this)
